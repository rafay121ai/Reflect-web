How it works
1

Notice what's on your mind

Start with a thought, a feeling, or something that happened. There's no wrong way to begin.

2

Answer a few questions

Gentle prompts help you look at your thought from different angles. Take your time.

3

Receive a reflection

A short mirror of what you shared—not advice, just your thoughts seen clearly.

4

Name how you feel, if you'd like

Optionally note your mood. Then carry something forward, or simply let it be.
