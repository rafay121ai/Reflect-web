# iReflect — SEO Keyword Strategy
_Target: English-speaking Western markets (US, UK, AU, CA)_
_ICP: Emotionally exhausted young working women seeking self-understanding_

---

## Primary Keyword

**`self-reflection app`**

**Why this keyword:**
- Exact category match — what the product IS, not a metaphor for it
- Lower competition than "journaling app" (Daylio, Day One dominate that term)
- High intent: people searching "self-reflection app" are ready to download something
- ICP language: emotionally aware users frame their need as "reflection", not "journaling"
- Searches like "best self-reflection app", "self-reflection app for women", "self-reflection app free" all cluster around this root

**Estimated monthly search volume:** 1,000–5,000 (US)
**Competition:** Medium-low (most results are listicles, not product pages)

## ICP Search Journey Map

```
PROBLEM AWARE
"why do I feel overwhelmed all the time"
"how to understand my emotions"
"how to be more self aware"
        ↓
SOLUTION AWARE
"self reflection app"
"app for self awareness"
"guided journaling app"
        ↓
PRODUCT AWARE
"AI journaling app"
"self reflection app free trial"
"iReflect app review"
```

iReflect's landing page should capture the **solution-aware** stage — users who know they want something, but haven't found the right product yet.

## Competitive Keyword Gap

| Competitor | Their keyword strength | Our angle |
|------------|----------------------|-----------|
| Daylio | mood tracking, habit tracker | We're not a tracker — we're a mirror |

**Our whitespace:** "self-reflection app that doesn't give advice" — no competitor owns this exact positioning.
